"Sneakers Store" Website Design

Project Detail

This website design was created as a dedicated platform for sneakers enthusiasts to browse and purchase their favorite shoes online. The design focuses on delivering a seamless user experience by showcasing products clearly, displaying prices prominently, and providing a detailed product page with descriptions, available sizes, and purchase buttons.

Currently, this design is still in the development stage. In the future, it may be realized as a fully functional website where users can directly make purchases.
